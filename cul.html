Cultural Committee

Our Vision

Appreciating the rich multicultural and multi linguistic tradition of Indian Society & understanding the true meaning of “Unity in Diversity'' is one of our main aims. We strive to capture the vibrant persona of the students by offering them a platform to showcase their inner musicians, dancers, actors, leaders & dreamers. We aim at making sure every event we host lasts in people’s memory for a lifetime. We, here at the Cultural Committee are one big family who not only works together but also has fun and hangs out together. Students getting selected for the Cultural Committee become an important part of this family. They are the backbone and are the people behind organizing all these events throughout the year. Every year our goals only keep getting bigger and we strive to take this committee to heights above and beyond.

Flagship Events

Cultural Night The flagship and the most awaited night for the entire college. A night where all the students of MPSTME are dressed in ethnic traditional attire and dance and sing to the DJ’s tunes. Rose and Chocolate Day the day of the event students can write letters to their dear friends appreciating them.

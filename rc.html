Research Cell

Vision

When time is on its course to change, there are those who watch and there are those who act. Here at Research Cell, we follow the spirit of both. We cause, analyze, and embrace change in all its glorious forms. In our pursuit to make "research" less intimidating for a community that would instead pick up codes from Stack Overflow than go through the documentation, we strive to introduce its very essence to the student community. Our engaging events and workshops not only expand academic barriers but also push them towards exploring interesting and in-demand skills required to carry themselves forward into the mesmerizing world of "Eureka"s! Endeavoring towards creating change, and improvising on what's done before - all while ensuring that our diligence translates to you, our true beneficiaries. With every challenge that the Research Cell takes on, we are driven by our understanding of our purpose of providing both, direction to your cause and ideas, and a platform to showcase it on.

Facebook Developer Circle
Participants take part in online discussions and attend local meet-ups by joining one of hundreds of circles around the globe.When innovators connect and collaborate, amazing things can happen. From building breakthrough solutions, to opportunities promoting diversity and inclusion.

Bombay Research Summit
The Bombay Research Summit is a 3-day conference for developers, both proficient and budding alike. It is a platform to engage with the like minded. We aim to connect with numerous industry experts from all over the world in the field of technology through this virtual meet conference. We also intend to encourage interaction and networking between the numerous communities, who are connected by passion, but separated by coordinates.

The Mentor Mentee Program
The Mentor Mentee Program is an initiative which will help students write a research paper or develop a project along with a research mentor constantly by your side. We connect you with a faculty member of your chosen subject and domain who will guide you and mentor you on every step of the journey.

Google Machine Learning Workshop
Workshop covering crowdsourcing and machine learning.

ResCon 21'
Case study competition, where a case study is given and the student has to find a solution to that case , and the most productive and effective answer wins.

Research Roulette
Seminar by researchers, followed by roulette wheel spun and students assigned to write a 800 word essay on the topic.

Research Fair
Covering one branch of our college's offered B.Tech branches per day, we intend to inform the attendees of the past, current, and prospective developments and innovations in the field whose seminars they wish to attend. In addition, every day's seminar can be followed with an industry-expert-led talk regarding the various avenues where students and graduates can intern in or work, hence ensuring that we're able to target a larger audience with this endeavour.

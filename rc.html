Research Cell

Our Vision

When time is on its course to change, there are those who watch and there are those who act. Here at Research Cell, we follow the spirit of both. We cause, analyze, and embrace change in all its glorious forms. In our pursuit to make "research" less intimidating for a community that would instead pick up codes from Stack Overflow than go through the documentation, we strive to introduce its very essence to the student community. Our engaging events and workshops not only expand academic barriers but also push them towards exploring interesting and in-demand skills required to carry themselves forward into the mesmerizing world of "Eureka"s! Endeavoring towards creating change, and improvising on what's done before - all while ensuring that our diligence translates to you, our true beneficiaries. With every challenge that the Research Cell takes on, we are driven by our understanding of our purpose of providing both, direction to your cause and ideas, and a platform to showcase it on.

Flagship Events

Facebook Developer Circleparticipants take part in online discussions and attend local meet-ups by joining one of hundreds of circles around the globe.When innovators connect and collaborate, amazing things can happen. From building breakthrough solutions, to opportunities promoting diversity and inclusion. The Bombay Research Summit is a 3-day conference for developers, both proficient and budding alike. It is a platform to engage with the like minded. We aim to connect with numerous industry experts from all over the world in the field of technology through this virtual meet conference. We also intend to encourage interaction and networking between the numerous communities, who are connected by passion, but separated by coordinates. The Mentor Mentee Program is an initiative which will help students write a research paper or develop a project along with a research mentor constantly by your side. We connect you with a faculty member of your chosen subject and domain who will guide you and mentor you on every step of the journey.

ISA

Our Vision

The International Society of Automation (ISA) is a non-profit professional association of engineers, technicians, and management engaged in industrial automation. As the globally trusted provider of foundational standards-based technical resources for the profession, ISA strives to build a better world through automation. At the MPSTME student's section of ISA we work tirelessly to achieve this goal through the various events and projects that we conduct throughout the year.

Flagship Events

This year our flagship event is the India Automation Games, 2022. It is the second time this event is being conducted in India and we are hosting it for the second time in a row under the guidance of ISA Maharashtra and ISA District G. The purpose of the event is to provide students with the opportunity to apply their knowledge in practical Automation and Robotics based scenarios and to let students meet, network and collaborate with other individuals with similar interests. The participants can interact with other students from the automation field who will be coming to our college from all over India.

Findrome

Our Vision

The finance cell of MPSTME Student body which organises events and activities such as workshops, guest lecturers from industry experts, business quiz, virtual stock market, management simulation games, case study competition and panel discussions in the financial domain.

Flagship Events

Aims at conducting activities and events that promote a culture to promote finance, equip students with the requisite financial expertise and help them make effective contributions to the growth of the corporate world. Events hosted by Findrome are Treasure Hunt, Panel Discussions, FinPlay and Corporate Shark Tank.

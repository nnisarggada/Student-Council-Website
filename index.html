OUR VISION

The Council

The university aims to offer a medium for the holistic development of students. The students are encouraged to build upon their skills and enhance their capabilities throughout their term.

Voices

Awareness is the basis of knowledge and teaching the youth to open their minds to crucial topics for healthy discussions. The college organizes many such seminars where experts or educated people in a myriad of fields are brought in to help the students learn more.

Social Work

It is important to give back to society and create a healthy environment for us. The university believes in providing for the underprivileged and those who need help through donations and other activities planned for helping the poor with rations and other facilities.

Sports

SVKM'S NMIMS MPSTME believes in nurturing creativity and keeping our students in touch with their hobbies. Students that have a keen interest in sports are offered multiple opportunities to pursue it through many sports-related activities and events organized.

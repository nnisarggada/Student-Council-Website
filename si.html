Social Impact

Our Vision

The team with a heart, ‘Social Impact’, is the social responsibility club of NMIMS MPSTME that strives to sensitize the future leaders of the institute towards social responsibilities. As students at one of the best universities in the country, we have a tremendous opportunity to steer the course of the future by carefully planning what we want to care about, where we want to give, and how we want to make an impact. We are here with a vision 'to care'; be it for our students and their mental health, for our teachers and their health care, for the NGOs and organisations in need of any form of assistance, to those on ground in need— Social Impact aims 'to care' for our people from all walks of life.

Flagship Events

Serve Out Smiles is our flagship event at SI, where we tie up with various NGOs, and offer students a chance to earn a certificate for social service by working with those NGOs for a stipulated time. Unwind is a three day national conference, backed by the United Nations, where students and professionals from around the country come together to discuss and find solutions for social issues that plague the nation.

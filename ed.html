Editorial Board

Our Vision

Through our events we work towards establishing a community for the creatives- the people who seek refuge in art and literature.

Flagship Events

Open Mic Night A night filled with soulful singers musicians and melodious poetry; With a twist of Skits and Stand ups. Blind Date with a Book Choosing atleast one book on the basis of 5 adjectives that shall replace the conventional book description, without knowing the title, cover or the author. Open to all genres. The Editorial Project A 3-day classical Lit-Fest experience with various events. TalkFest on the 10th of every month, the Editorial Board brings you a new pioneer in the world of art and literature via an interactive Q&A. Crossfire Debate Conference: wherein we simulate riveting debates on the latest and greatest in the world, ranging from discussions about human rights, to political affairs.
